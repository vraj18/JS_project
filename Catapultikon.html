What is Catapultikon?

Welcome to the ultimate test of engineering, strategy, and athleticism! Catapultikon Challenge invites teams to design, build, and launch catapults in a thrilling competition inspired by the spirit of the Olympics. Just like Olympic athletes, participants will push the boundaries of their creativity and skill to construct catapults that can accurately and powerfully launch projectiles. From the initial blueprint to the final launch, teams will face challenges that require teamwork, problem-solving, and a relentless pursuit of victory.

How the Event is Conducted

  1. Round 1:This round evaluates participants on three key criteria:
  2. Creativity. 5th, 6th and 7th
  3. Precision. 8th, 9th and 10th
  4. Knowledge. 11th and 12th class
  5. Round 2: Bullseye BlitzContestants will be timed to determine the number of successful launches within a given timeframe.
  6. Round 3: Victory LapIn the final round, participants will apply their understanding of catapult mechanics to accurately hit a target.

Rules

  • 1. Maximum of 3 members are alowed in one team from 6th to 12th standard.
  • 2. No equipment will be provided at the venue & you will have to bring your Catapult.
  • 3. The participant must bring the letter of acceptance on the day of even
  • 4. In case of any dispute, the decision of the organising committee will be final and binding.
  • 3. The maximum size of the catapult can be 3x3x3 feet and it should not be handheld.
  • 3. Only mechanical and non-electrical arrangements are allowed in mechanism & Catapult should have only one arm.
  • The Catapult must be able to fire any spherical projectile with a maximum diameter of 4 cm.
